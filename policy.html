Privacy Policy

At MindWell, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly as well as automatically when you interact with our website.

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact information
  • Appointment details (preferred date, time, and message)
  • Any additional information you provide when submitting forms, including appointment scheduling

Non-Personal Information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Date/time of access

We collect non-personal information through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and the functionality of our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To schedule and manage appointments with our mental health professionals.
  • To send appointment confirmations, updates, and responses to your inquiries.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • To send you relevant content and updates if you have subscribed to our newsletter or services (with your explicit consent).
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends to improve our services.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard measures, including physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

However, please understand that no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
